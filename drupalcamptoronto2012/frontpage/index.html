Top Ten Reasons to Attend DrupalCamp Toronto 2012

DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 is shaping up to an amazing event for Drupal enthusiasts. Here are ten reasons why you won't want to miss it!

10. It's Inexpensive!

Similar conferences of this scale in Toronto are expensive - in the range of $500 and higher. DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 costs a low $65 for the entire two-day event! Considering the huge variety of sessions, great keynotes and community interaction, DrupalCamp is a huge bargain! We also have student discounts!

9. Networking

DrupalCamp is a great event to connect with developers, employers and other Drupal enthusiasts. You'll have countless opportunities to connect during the event and at our opening night party.

8. Keynote by Jeff Eaton

Jeff Eaton is widely know as one of the most engaging and entertaining speakers in the Drupal community. If you've ever learned about platypodes, bets are that you can thank Jeff.

7. Birds of a Feather

What is a DrupalCamp without the BoF sessions? BoFs are a chance for you to create your own informal sessions on topics not covered in our main program of sessions. Share your knowledge and/or questions informally with fellow Drupal enthusiasts!

6. 25+ Sessions

The heart of DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 is the 25+ sessions we're hosting on Friday and Saturday. We'll have a range of sessions for beginner, intermediate and advanced skill levels covering tracks for development, design and business.

5. Great Prizes!

We have some great prizes for attendees to win! Attendees have a chance to win a 1-year Drupalize.me membership (a $420 value), a 1-year premium Lynda.com subscription (a $450 value) and DrupalCamp travel mugs!

4. Keynote by James Walker

James Walker has worked with Drupal for a very long time. His reputation as a proponent of open source technologies, being a skilled educator and community leadership are well earned. Trivia: James actually helped organize the first DrupalCamp events in Toronto!

3. Great Sponsors

DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 wouldn't be possible without the generous support of our amazing sponsors! Thank you to Peapod Studios, Myplanet Digital, Freeform Solutions, Digital Echidna, Nextide, 2bits.com, Evolving Web, JMA Consulting, Lullabot, K2 Digital, Discovery Garden and Verge Interactive for their sponsorship of this year's event.

2. Keynote by Angela Byron

Angela Byron is the core co-maintainer of Drupal 7, an award winning developer and author. She lives and breathes Drupal like few others. Angela was the first woman to be featured on the cover of Linux Journal, in April 2011.

1. The Community

Drupal's best strength is its community. The opportunities to meet up with fellow DrupalCamp attendees is invaluable. Whether it's impromptu conversations between sessions, Birds of a Feather sessions, mingling at the opening night party or elsewhere, you'll quickly learn why the Drupal community rocks!

So there you have it! Head over to the registration page and sign-up for DrupalCamp Toronto 2012!
