Location

The 2011 DrupalCamp Toronto will be held on November 12-13 at the St. James campus of George Brown College, located at 290 Adelaide Street East. To get there by subway, exit at King subway station, then walk or take the King streetcar east to Jarvis Street, and walk one block east on King Street. Parking is available on campus.
