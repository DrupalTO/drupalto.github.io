DrupalCamp Toronto 2011

DrupalCamp Toronto is a BarCamp style unconference focused on sharing skills and knowledge about Drupal. Our goal is to bring together smart people in an environment that is welcoming to all.

We have created several purchase points for tickets since this event is focused on sharing knowledge and bringing new faces into our community. We ask for $35 per day, but if you can’t meet that price please pay what you can.

This is a low cost, volunteer run event and so we encourage everyone to get involved. Propose a session, sponsor a small amount or simply pick up after yourself during the day. We set the stage, but all of you make this happen!
