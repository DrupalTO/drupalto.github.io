Welcome!

The second (annual?) DrupalCampToronto is happening in May. Find out all you need to know here!

When?

May 11 - 12. That's Friday and Saturday. We'll start at 9am and go until we can't go anymore.

Where?

We'll be at the Bahen Centre at the University of Toronto.

40 St. George Street
Toronto, ON (of course)

Register!

Register now! We have space for around 150 people, so make sure you get in before then. And please don't take spots if you're not going to show up. Thanks :)

Present!

Once you've registered please propose a session - the deadline is May 4th

Also check out the other proposed sessions, read through them and vote on your favourites!
