Drupal Camp Toronto 2010: October 15-16

If you are seeing this, you are still visiting the Drupalcamp 2008 site.

The next Drupal Camp Toronto is being planned for the weekend of October 15-16, 2010.

Welcome to DrupalCampToronto 2008!


It's that time of year again! May is coming, which means we're doing another DrupalCampToronto!

When

Friday, May 23rd and Saturday May 24th, 2008. 9am to 5pm.

Where

We'll be at the University of Toronto in the Bissell Building.

140 St. George St
Toronto, ON

map

Register!

Register now! We have space for around 150 people, so make sure you get in before then. And please don't take spots if you're not going to show up. Thanks :)

Present!

Once you've registered please propose a session - the deadline is May 19th

Also check out the other proposed sessions, read through them and vote on your favourites!

Sponsor!

If you're part of Drupal-related business or organization, Drupal Camp is a great opportunity to advertise your services, build networks, and learn more -- and you'll want to sponsor this event. Contact us when you're ready - but make it now if you want your logo on the t-shirt!
