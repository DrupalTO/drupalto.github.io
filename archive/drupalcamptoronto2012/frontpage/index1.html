Get Ready to Vote for Your Favourite Sessions!

We've had dozens of session submissions in the past couple of weeks! Thanks to everyone in the community who has submitted a session.

A range of beginner, intermediate and advanced have been proposed by the community; an exciting program is coming together for DrupalCamp Toronto 2012!

Voting will open later today, so check out the current list of session proposals and get your voting mojo ready!

Session submissions will close once voting begins, so if you're still on the fence about submitting a session, get in contact with us and we can help you out. We're still open to having more beginner session proposals, so if you have a great idea for new Drupal users, let us know!

DrupalCamp Loves Peapod Studios!

Thanks to Peapod Studios (a platinum sponsor) for donating several new graphic elements to DrupalCamp Toronto 2012. Our original volunteer designer left the project early and our friends at Peapod Studios stepped up to complete the job with all new banners, images, logos and social media elements for our website, Facebook, Twitter, T-shirts, swag bags and signs. Their work is not only visually beautiful, but it was also delivered with lightning speed.

Check out Peapod Studios' blog post "Peapod Loves DrupalCamp Toronto" on their escapepod blog.

Read more about Peapod Studios on our dedicated platinum sponsor page.
