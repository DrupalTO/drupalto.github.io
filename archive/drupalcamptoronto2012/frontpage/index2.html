Submit a Session!

DrupalCamp is fast approaching and we're ready to accept session submissions from the community!

Do you have a great idea for a session? Do you have some Drupal knowledge to share with your fellow Drupal enthusiasts? Then head on over to our session submission page to submit a session.

Note: you must be signed in to submit a session!

We've already received some session proposals, which can be viewed on the Proposed Sessions page. New sessions will be added daily, so check back often to see what's being proposed by the community.

Past sessions have included:

  • An Introduction to Drupal
  • Multilingual Site Buiding With Drupal 7
  • Site Performance, Optimization & Scalability
  • How Drupal Enables Designers
  • Education & Community Site Case Studies
  • Agile Practices for Drupal
  • Winning in the Enterprise With Drupal
  • Responsive Design and Drupal Theming
  • Pixel Perfect: Drupal Slideshows and Photo Galleries
  • Drupal SEO

...and many more!

Get Ready for DrupalCamp Toronto 2012!

Welcome to the new DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 website!

Your organizing team has been hard at work over the past few weeks in preparation for hosting our annual DrupalCamp Toronto event. This year's camp is happening on November 16th & 17th at The Burroughes Building, a beautifully renovated space, at the corner of Queen and Bathurst.

DrupalCamp Toronto 2012 promises to be an exciting event, with great sessions for designers and developers, individuals and businesses, and beginner to expert level Drupal users.

Our keynote speakers for this year's event are Angie Byron and James Walker!

This year's core organizing team from the Toronto Drupal User Group includes:

  • Chris Luckhardt (@motionblur)
  • Steph Brown (@vicearoni)
  • Tyler Struyk (@iStryker)
  • Emad Al-Shihabi (@emadshi)
  • Teresa Chin (@teresajchin)
  • Cleaver Barnes (@cleaverbarnes)
  • Aidan Foster (@finteractive)

The full list of community members who are assisting with this year's DrupalCamp will be announced soon!

Registration will open up in the next few days! You'll also be able to submit your session proposals soon too! Watch for registration and session submission announcements here or via our Twitter (hashtag: #dcto2012) and Facebook social media channels.
