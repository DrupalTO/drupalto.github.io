Software fit for human consumption

Submitted by mtabini on Tue, 2011-10-25 16:15
3
Log in to vote
Session Track: 
Business and Strategy
Difficulty: 
Intermediate

As tools become more powerful and computing power more accessible, the biggest challenge that developers face is that of creating software that truly caters to its users' need for usability and problem solving.

Yet, much of the focus of engineering is, at all levels, projected on the wrong issues: automation instead of knowledge, form instead of function, and quantity instead of quality.

In this talk, Marco will give you a tour of what makes software work for the user and show you with three simple examples that user experience design is the domain of tomorrow's software engineer.
