What’s the right image solution for my site?

Submitted by jsfwd on May 13, 2008 - 4:36am.

There are many different approaches to handle images on your site, but which one is the best approach for yours?

This session will cover the pros/cons of different solutions that are out there in relation to the needs of your site.

For the most part, I’m creating this session because when I first started with Drupal, what was the best approach would often make me want to bang my head against my desk. So, to prevent any future concussions, I’d like to pass along my experiences with different modules.

If there is anything in particular you are interested in with this topic, leave a comment and I’ll do my best to include it.
