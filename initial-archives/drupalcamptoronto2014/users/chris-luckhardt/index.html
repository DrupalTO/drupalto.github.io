Bio

I'm a Toronto-based Drupal Specialist, primarily focusing on system architecture, design, development, theming, site building, content management and project management.

I've been working with Drupal for over 6 years.

I'm very active in the Toronto Drupal community, helping to promote Drupal as one of the directors of the non-profit Toronto Drupal User Group. We organize monthly Drupal meetups, DrupalCamp Toronto and other events throughout the year for the Toronto Drupal community.

Apart from Drupal, I'm also an active photographer, with recent features on ABC News, BuzzFeed, Flickr's Blog, Twisted Sifter and TotallyCoolPix.

I'm also learning Japanese and have an obsession with guitars.
