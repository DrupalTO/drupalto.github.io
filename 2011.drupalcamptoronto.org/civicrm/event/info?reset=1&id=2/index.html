DrupalCamp Toronto is a BarCamp style unconference focused on sharing skills and knowledge about Drupal. Our goal is to bring together smart people in an environment that is welcoming to all.

Since this event is focused on sharing knowledge and bringing new faces into our community, we have created several purchase points for tickets. We ask for $35 per day, but if you can’t meet that price please pay what you can.

Since this is a low cost, volunteer run event we encourage everyone to get involved. Propose a session, sponsor a small amount or simply pick up after yourself during the day. We set the stage, but all of you make this happen!
