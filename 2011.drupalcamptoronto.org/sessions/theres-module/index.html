There's a Module for That

Submitted by emmajane on Thu, 2011-10-20 11:26
9
Log in to vote
Session Track: 
Drupal for Beginners
Difficulty: 
Beginner

The hardest part of learning to build a Drupal site is choosing the right combination of modules for today--and tomorrow's version of what your Web site will become. Reading through the entire list of all Drupal modules is not the way to choose the module that's matches your needs; nor should you automatically assume you have to program your own modules to build your Web site.

In this whirlwind tour Emma Jane will list the top 50 (or so) modules you definitely need to know about to build powerful Drupal Web sites quickly. From tiny helper modules (like Menu Block) to massive social infrastructure modules (like Organic Groups) you can reduce your coding time by finding the perfect Drupal module for your site.

About The Presenter

Emma Jane Hogbin is well known in the Drupal community for her engaging presentations and kickass theming book, Front End Drupal. Her latest book, Drupal: A User's Guide, presents a decade of site building experience in an easy-to-read volume. (Yes, there will be a few free copies given away at the presentation.) With contributions in Drupal's Building Blocks by Earl and Lynette Miles as well as her own workbooks, and a GPL Druplicon knitting pattern, Emma Jane earns her money the hard way: by teaching others how to profit from her experience.
